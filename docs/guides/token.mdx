# Create a Token DAO

You can create a DAO around an existing token or you can mint a new token. Follow the steps below to create your token DAO. 
## Prerequisites

Before you can create a Token DAO, make sure you have completed the following:

- Download Google Chrome
- Install [the Station Chrome Extension](https://setup.station.money/)

## Connect your wallet to Enterprise

1. Visit [https://dao.enterprise.money/](https://dao.enterprise.money/). 

2. To connect your wallet to the Enterprise DAO site, click **Connect Wallet** in the upper-right corner of the screen. 

<img className="readme-img-md" src="/img/screens/token/setup-1.png" />

3. Select **Station Wallet**. 

<img className="readme-img-md" src="/img/screens/token/setup-2.png" />

4. Click **approve** in the Station extension. 

<img className="readme-img-sm" src="/img/screens/token/setup-3.png" />


You are now connected to your wallet. Before you continue, make sure your network is set to the **mainnet**.

## Create a Token DAO

1. Click the **Create DAO** button in the upper-right corner of the screen. 

<img className="readme-img-md" src="/img/screens/token/setup-4.png" />

2. Select **Token DAO**. 

<img className="readme-img-md" src="/img/screens/token/setup-5.png" />

3. Input a name for your DAO, a logo URL, and a description. The logo URL must be the direct URL path of an image, not an HTML page. Click **Next**.

<img className="readme-img-md" src="/img/screens/token/setup-6.png" />

## Create a new token

The next steps outline how to create a new token for your DAO. Skip to the [next section](#use-an-existing-token) to create a DAO around an existing token. 


1. Click **No, create a new token** and then click **Next**. 

<img className="readme-img-md" src="/img/screens/token/setup-new-1.png" />


2. Enter your token parameters, including, a token name, a token symbol, the number of decimal places your token can be denominated in a logo URL, and a token description. Click **Next**.

<img className="readme-img-md" src="/img/screens/token/setup-new-2.png" />


4. Enter the wallet addresses you want to receive the initial distribution of tokens. Be sure to specify the amount each wallet will receive. You can also supply the DAO treasury with tokens. Remember to give yourself an initial balance, otherwise you won't be able to stake or vote in the DAO. Click **Next**.

<img className="readme-img-md" src="/img/screens/token/setup-new-3.png" />

## Use an existing token

The next steps outline how to create a DAO around an existing token. If you created a new token, you can skip to the next section. 

1. Click **Yes, find my token** and then search for your token. The list will have all Station-supported CW-20 or native tokens on any chain that has integrated Enterprise DAO. You can view all supported token denominations by visiting the [Station asset repo](https://github.com/terra-money/station-assets). 

<img className="readme-img-md" src="/img/screens/token/setup-ex-2.png" />

2. Click **Next**


## Configure the DAO's settings

1. Define your [vote duration](../concepts/config.mdx#vote-duration), [quorum](../concepts/config.mdx#quorum), [threshold](../concepts/config.mdx#threshold), [veto threshold](../concepts/config.mdx#veto-threshold), and [unlocking period](../concepts/config.mdx#unlocking-period) parameters. You can also specify the minimum number of staked tokes needed to vote, and a minimum required to recieve rewards. 

<Admonition type="caution" title="Updating parameters">

After creation, [DAO parameters](../concepts/config.mdx) can only be updated using a [governance proposal](./governance.mdx). 

</Admonition>


<img className="readme-img-md" src="/img/screens/token/setup-7.png" />

2. Click **Next**.

## Add council members

DAO council members can create emergency proposals to update the DAO's contract, whitelist, or information without having to go through regular governance. 

1. Add the wallet addresses of your council members, and specify a threshold and quorum number for council votes. 

<img className="readme-img-md" src="/img/screens/token/setup-8.png" />

2. Click **Next**. 



## Final steps

1. Enter any social media links you wish to add. Click **Next**.

<img className="readme-img-md" src="/img/screens/token/setup-9.png" />

2. Review all the settings for your DAO carefully and click **Next**. 

<img className="readme-img-md" src="/img/screens/token/setup-10.png" />

<Admonition type="caution" title="Review your parameters">

Remember, the only way to change these parameters is through a [governance proposal](./governance.mdx). 

</Admonition>


3. Review the transaction in Station and click **Post**.

<img className="readme-img-md" src="/img/screens/token/setup-11.png" />

Congratulations! You've just made a Token DAO!
